Dear EHR Randomizer User:

Starting October 2, we are asking users to direct all inquiries to the ONC JIRA Instance. The Test EHR Program has moved all communications to JIRA in an effort to provide improved efficiency of service.

In order to submit an inquiry or search existing inquiries, please create an account.
This is a simple process and takes less than a minute:

  1. Click the link to begin registration: Randomizer JIRA Instance
  2. Click “Log-In” on the top right corner, then click “Sign Up” to create an account.
  3. Log in with your new Username and Password.
JIRA also makes it easy to search existing inquiries and responses for an answer to your question, and start new inquiries if you can’t find a similar question.
  1. To search existing inquiries, click “Issues” on the left side menu:
    1. Select “All Issues” from the top menu
    2. View issues by title on the left, or type your search term into the box and click “Search”
    3. If an inquiry of interest is still awaiting response, click “Watch Issue” to receive updates when the inquiry is updated
  2. If you can’t find an inquiry similar to your own, create a new inquiry:
    1. To create a new inquiry, click the orange “Create Issue” button at the top of screen to enter your inquiry.
Thank you in advance for your participation and helping us improve our response capacity! For additional questions, please refer to the JIRA FAQs.
